Client Experience

We offer sound advice and support before, during and after significant life transitions to help you and your family to navigate life’s expected and unexpected events. You can rely on Fairport Asset Management.

Set Up a Discovery Meeting

Our Typical Client Has the Following Attributes

  • Net worth of at least $2 million
  • $1 million or more of investable assets
  • Willingness to share important and sometimes sensitive details of their lives, goals, aspirations and concerns
  • Willingness to delegate to a trusted advisor
  • Seeking comprehensive solutions, not just investment management
  • Experiencing a life transition
  • Has realistic expectations and lives within their means
